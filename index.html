Craft Beer Analysis

Welcome to our analysis on craft beer and their breweries! The aim of this data analysis was to analyze what style of craft beer are most popular and where they come from.

This data is useful for a wide range of commercial applications, such as marketing, development, or if you own a brewery, whats popular in your area!

Try exploring our API to find what you need! All raw data that can be used for further analysis; aswell as contact info for the developers and data analysists, can be found under the "Data" section of our site.
